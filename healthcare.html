TOP THREATS TO HEALTHCARE

What's Targeting Your Industry

Ransomware Attacks on Hospital Systems

Attackers encrypt patient records and clinical systems, shutting down hospital operations. A ransomware attack can cost healthcare facilities millions in downtime and regulatory penalties.

Patient Data Breaches

Attacks specifically targeting patient records, medical histories, and personal health information. A single breach can compromise millions of patient records, leading to massive HIPAA fines.

Medical Device Exploitation

Connected medical devices (monitors, infusion pumps, diagnostic equipment) are vulnerable to attacks. Compromise of medical devices can directly impact patient safety.

Insider Threats

Hospital staff and contractors with access to patient systems can abuse privileges or become victims of social engineering, intentionally or accidentally exposing sensitive patient data.

Supply Chain Attacks

Attacks on healthcare vendors, EHR providers, and medical device manufacturers can compromise entire hospital networks. Third-party vulnerabilities are difficult to control but have wide-reaching impact.

Compliance Audit Failures

HIPAA audits reveal gaps in access controls, encryption, and incident response. Audit failures lead to corrective action plans and potential OCR (Office for Civil Rights) investigations.

Education & Research Security

IP Protection & Student Data Security

1,000+ educational institutions breached in 2024
$2.5M average cost of a research IP theft incident

Universities and research institutions store vast amounts of sensitive data: student records, financial information, and groundbreaking intellectual property. Open campus networks, bring‑your‑own‑device policies, and collaborative research environments create unique security challenges. Attackers target educational institutions for data theft, ransomware, and espionage.

Rezolute specializes in protecting education and research with FERPA compliance, IP protection, and campus network security tailored to academic environments.

TOP THREATS TO EDUCATION & RESEARCH

What's Targeting Your Industry

Intellectual Property Theft

Nation‑state actors and competitors target research data, patents, and unpublished findings. Loss of IP can cost millions and destroy competitive advantage.

Student Data Breaches

Personal information, financial aid records, and academic histories are prime targets for identity theft and fraud. FERPA violations lead to loss of federal funding.

Ransomware on Campus

Ransomware attacks encrypt student records, research data, and administrative systems, halting operations and demanding large payments from already‑stretched budgets.

Unsecured Campus Networks

Open Wi‑Fi, guest access, and thousands of personal devices create a sprawling attack surface. Lateral movement from compromised student devices is common.

Insider Threats & Accidental Exposure

Students, faculty, and staff may inadvertently expose sensitive data through misconfigured cloud storage or phishing. Insider threats are difficult to monitor.

Regulatory Compliance Gaps

Failure to meet FERPA, GDPR (for European students), or research grant security requirements can result in fines, loss of funding, and reputational damage.

Our Academic Security Services

Research IP Protection

We help research institutions safeguard intellectual property with data classification, access controls, and monitoring tailored to collaborative environments. Our solutions ensure that sensitive research data remains confidential while enabling authorised collaboration.

Research Data Classification

Identify and label sensitive research data (CUI, ITAR, proprietary) with appropriate handling requirements.

Secure Collaboration Platforms

Implement encrypted file sharing and virtual research environments that protect IP during collaboration.

Export Control Compliance

Controls to prevent unauthorised access to ITAR/EAR‑controlled technical data by foreign nationals.

Student Data Privacy & FERPA Compliance

We ensure your institution meets FERPA requirements with robust access controls, audit trails, and data protection measures. Our solutions help you manage directory information, respond to data subject requests, and demonstrate compliance.

FERPA Gap Analysis

Comprehensive assessment of current controls against FERPA requirements with remediation roadmap.

Student Information System (SIS) Security

Hardening and monitoring of SIS platforms to prevent unauthorised access and data leaks.

Data Breach Response

Incident response procedures that satisfy FERPA breach notification requirements and minimise harm.

Campus Network & Endpoint Security

University networks are notoriously open. We implement network segmentation, NAC (Network Access Control), and endpoint protection to contain threats and secure both wired and wireless infrastructure.

Network Access Control (NAC)

Enforce security policies for all devices connecting to the campus network – student, faculty, guest, and IoT.

Wi‑Fi Security & Segmentation

Isolate guest traffic from internal networks and implement WPA3‑Enterprise with 802.1X authentication.

Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)

Deploy EDR across university‑managed devices to detect and respond to threats in real time.

Research Grant Security (CMMC / NIST 800-171)

Many research grants now require compliance with NIST 800‑171 or CMMC. We help you implement the necessary security controls to protect Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) and maintain eligibility for federal funding.

NIST 800‑171 Assessment

Evaluate your environment against the 110 security requirements and develop a System Security Plan (SSP).

CMMC Readiness

Prepare for CMMC certification at the appropriate level (L1–L3) with documented policies and technical controls.

CUI Data Management

Implement data‑loss prevention and access controls specifically for Controlled Unclassified Information.

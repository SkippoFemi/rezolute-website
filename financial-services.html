Rezolute specializes in protecting financial institutions with regulatory compliance, data protection, and enterprise security tailored to the financial sector.

TOP THREATS TO FINANCIAL SERVICES

What's Targeting Your Industry

Credential Theft & Account Takeover

Attackers target employee and customer credentials to gain unauthorized access to financial systems. Stolen credentials enable fraudulent transactions and data theft.

Money Laundering & Fraud Systems Exploitation

Criminals exploit insecure financial systems to launder money and conduct fraud. Weak controls enable millions in illicit transfers.

Insider Trading & Data Exfiltration

Employees with access to confidential financial data can abuse privileges for personal gain. Data exfiltration leads to competitive loss and regulatory violations.

ATM/Payment System Compromise

Attacks on payment processing systems and ATM networks can disrupt customer transactions and drain accounts. Payment system downtime costs millions hourly.

Regulatory Audit Failures

SOX, GLBA, and regulatory audits reveal gaps in controls and compliance. Audit failures result in corrective action plans, fines, and reputational damage.

Third-Party/Vendor Breaches

Compromised vendors and third-party services can breach financial institutions. Vendor risk management failures expose customer data and systems.
